# Swedish annual-report readiness scope

This feature is a guided readiness and learning layer for Swedish limited companies. It is not yet statutory filing software.

## Standard deadline

All Swedish limited companies must prepare and submit an annual report. The standard submission deadline is seven months after the fiscal year ends.

A continued general meeting can, in specific circumstances and with the required notification, result in a later submission deadline. The product must therefore describe seven months as the standard deadline rather than claiming that no legal exception exists.

## K2 eligibility

Many smaller limited companies can use K2, but eligibility must be checked. K2 is not available to every small company. Restrictions include public companies and certain parent companies, and the permitted scope changed for financial years beginning after 31 December 2025.

## Prototype controls

The current prototype includes:

- fiscal-year deadline calculation
- K2/K3 selection
- year-end close checklist
- bank, receivable, payable, VAT, payroll, fixed-asset, accrual, equity and tax checks
- balance-sheet reconciliation
- unmapped-account warnings
- negative-cash and negative-equity warnings
- bilingual product direction for non-expert founders

## Future filing product

Before digital filing is offered, the product needs:

- explicit K2 eligibility assessment
- current taxonomy and validation rules
- complete K2 financial statements, notes and management report
- comparative figures and rounding controls
- audit and auditor-report handling where applicable
- board approval and signature workflow
- immutable evidence trail
- integration with Bolagsverket's annual-report submission API
- legal and accounting review
